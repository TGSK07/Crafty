General Questions

What makes Crafty products unique?

All items sold on Crafty are authenticated handmade products sourced directly from local artisans across India. This ensures quality, originality, and direct support for the creator.

How can I become a seller on Crafty?

We welcome genuine artisans! You can sign up as a 'Seller' during registration or upgrade your profile later. We require verification of your craft and ethical sourcing practices.

Click here to start the registration process.

Order & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments through our secure gateway (Razorpay).

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Modifications or cancellations are possible only before the artisan confirms shipment (usually within 24 hours). Please contact support immediately with your order number.

The Crafty Story

Crafty is more than just a marketplace; it's a bridge connecting modern shoppers with the rich heritage and immense talent of local Indian artisans. Our mission is built on transparency, fair trade, and sustainability. We believe in the power of traditional craftsmanship to inspire and enrich lives.

Empowering Artisans

We believe in fair wages and direct partnerships. By cutting out the middlemen, we ensure that the creators receive the true value of their meticulous work, supporting their families and traditional crafts.

Sustainable & Ethical

Every product on Crafty is handmade, often using sustainable materials and time-honored techniques. We champion ethical sourcing and environmentally conscious practices from production to delivery.

Bringing Culture Home

From Madhubani paintings to Block-print fabrics, each item carries a unique regional story. Our goal is to make these cultural treasures accessible to everyone, enriching homes with authentic artistry.

Meet the Crafty Founders

Crafty was conceived and developed as a passion project by us, 3rd Year B.Tech students from Sharda University, Greater Noida. We combined our love for technology with a vision to support India's vibrant artisan community.
